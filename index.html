about

baçura started in 2019 as an art project for founder and Creative Director; Samantha Doctora. Initially a passion project, baçura was conceived through late nights spent cogitating over a rapidly expanding body of designs and illustrations.

sustainability

definition: the ability to continue a defined behaviour indefinitely. Also known as the ability to maintain healthy & balanced environmental, social and economic systems on a global and local scale.

impact of fashion

Fashion is the second largest polluter in the world, just after the oil industry, and one of the largest consumers of water. The environmental damage is increasing as the industry grows. However, there are solutions to mitigate these problems.The first step lies in building awareness and willingness to change.

we see what we want jacket

say hello to the 'WSWWW jacket'. a functionally-orientated piece, cut from a premium cotton and features large waist patch pockets, bound internal seams, and an oversized button-up front closure. Designed to offer a clean, minimal light jacket dripped in functionality perfect for both casual and formal cross-seasonal wear.
